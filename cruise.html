Space Ratio = Gross Tonnage ÷ Passengers (double occupancy). Industry standard crowding metric — higher is less crowded. ≥44 = spacious · 42–43 = comfortable · below 42 = denser.
Nightly rate is a per-person ballpark estimate (interior cabin, 2-person occupancy) — not an all-in cost. Add gratuities, packages, flights, and excursions for true total.
